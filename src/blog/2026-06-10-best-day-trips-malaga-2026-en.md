# Best Day Trips from Malaga in 2026: Ronda, Nerja, Caminito del Rey & Hidden Gems

You wake up in central Malaga and by mid-morning you stand on the dramatic bridge in Ronda or walk the thrilling paths of Caminito del Rey. Excellent transport makes it easy to explore and return to Good Vibes Apartments each evening.

![Iconic Puente Nuevo bridge spanning the dramatic gorge in Ronda](/assets/img/blog/ronda-puente-nuevo-dramatic-gorge.jpg)

## Why Day Trips Work Perfectly from Central Malaga

You reach major stations in minutes. Leave after breakfast and return for sunset on your terrace.

## Ronda: Dramatic Cliffs and Timeless Beauty

The iconic Puente Nuevo, historic bullring, whitewashed streets, and mountain cuisine.

## Nerja: Caves, Balconies and Coastal Charm

Spectacular caves, the famous Balcón de Europa, beautiful coves for swimming.

![Stunning view from Balcon de Europa in Nerja overlooking the Mediterranean](/assets/img/blog/nerja-balcon-de-europa-sea-view.jpg)

## Caminito del Rey

Book in advance. The dramatic walkway is an unforgettable active day out.
