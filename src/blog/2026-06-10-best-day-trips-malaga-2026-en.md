## Caminito del Rey: The King’s Path Adventure from Good Vibes Apartments in Central Malaga

The Caminito del Rey has become one of Spain’s most thrilling day trips. The dramatic walkway clings to the steep walls of the Desfiladero de los Gaitanes.

Practical details for 2026:

- Advance online booking is essential.
- The walk itself takes 2–3 hours plus transport time.
- You return via a shuttle bus or taxi.
- Wear good shoes and bring water and sun protection.

From Good Vibes Apartments you reach the area in roughly 1 hour. It makes an unforgettable active day out when you choose the best malaga vacation apartments.
