# Eat Like a Local: The Complete Malaga Foodie Guide to Tapas, Seafood & Traditional Cuisine

You taste the real Malaga the moment you bite into perfectly grilled espetos or share boquerones in a tiny bar. Malaga’s food scene blends centuries-old recipes with the freshest Mediterranean ingredients.

![Vibrant display of fresh seafood and local produce at Malaga's bustling central market](/assets/img/blog/malaga-central-market-seafood-produce.jpg)

## The Soul of Malaga Cuisine

Andalusian cooking celebrates the sea and the land. Ultra-fresh seafood, olive oil, garlic, tomatoes, almonds, and honey.

## Must-Try Dishes

### Seafood Classics
- Espetos de sardinas grilled over wood fire
- Pescaíto frito
- Boquerones en vinagre
- Gazpachuelo

### Tapas & Small Plates
- Berenjenas con miel
- Tortillita de camarones

![Traditional Malaga tapas spread: fried eggplant with honey, anchovies and beer](/assets/img/blog/malaga-central-market-seafood-produce.jpg)

## Where Locals Actually Eat

Start at the Mercado Central de Atarazanas. Then traditional bars, bodegas, and chiringuitos on the coast.
